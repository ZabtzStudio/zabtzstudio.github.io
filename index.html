About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Zabtz Studios is an independent game development studio dedicated to creating unique and engaging gaming experiences. Our team of passionate developers, artists, and storytellers come together to craft worlds that captivate and inspire.

We believe in the power of games to tell meaningful stories, bring people together, and push the boundaries of interactive entertainment. Every pixel, every line of code, and every sound effect is carefully crafted with player experience in mind.

Our mission is to create games that stay with players long after they've put down the controller.
